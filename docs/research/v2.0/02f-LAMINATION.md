## 3. Step 1: The Superposition of Well Depths

In Paper **02b**, we proved that every bit is a physical well with an Energy ($E$) depth proportional to its mass ($M_\sigma$).

### 3.1 The Additive Property
According to the **Principle of Superposition** in classical physics, if two potential energy fields occupy the same coordinate, the total potential is the sum of the parts. 
*   **The Scenario:** We place two resonant bits ($A$ and $B$) on the same physical memory tile (`01b`). 
*   **The Physical Rule:** The two energy valleys merge into one.

### 3.2 The Derivation of Mass Addition
We define the energy of the combined state as the sum of individual depths:

$$E_{(A \oplus B)} = E_A + E_B$$

Substituting our mass formula ($E = M_\sigma E_{min}$):

$$M_{\sigma(A \oplus B)} = M_{\sigma A} + M_{\sigma B}$$

*Conclusion: Stacking layers increases the **Structural Inertia** ($M_\sigma$). The truth becomes "Heavier" and harder for external noise to flip or change.*

## 4. Step 2: The Geometry of Sharpening (Intersection)

In Paper **02a**, we defined the "Truth" of a bit as its residence within a safe **Neighborhood** ($V$).

### 4.2 The Shrinking of Uncertainty

$$V_{(A \oplus B)} = V_A \cap V_B$$
