## 3. The Diagnostic: The Fragmented Observer

Modern User Interfaces (UIs) treat the human user as a "Context Switcher," forcing the individual to bridge the logical gaps between weightless, disconnected applications.

### 3.1 The Silo Entrapment
As of 2026, the average professional navigates dozens of distinct applications daily. Because these apps do not share a **Causal Spine** (`01c`), a decision or data state in App-A is invisible to App-B. This forces the human to act as the "Manual Glue," a role that is highly prone to fatigue, data loss, and **Human Hallucination** (misremembering context across silos).

### 3.2 The Chatbot Proxy
Current AI assistants (e.g., Copilots or standalone LLMs) are merely new silos. They sit *above* the Operating System but cannot *perceive* the thermodynamic state of the data they process. They act as **Blind Guides**, summarizing text or generating plans without any physical mechanism to verify the **Source Consistency** (`01a`) of the underlying bits, as highlighted by the limitations of "Intrinsic Self-Critique" (**Bohnet et al., DeepMind, 2025**).
