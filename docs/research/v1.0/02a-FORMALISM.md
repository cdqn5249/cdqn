$$\rho_{ij}(\mathcal{L}_i) = \rho_{ji}(\mathcal{L}_j)$$

*   **Fluid Lattices** minimize tension through **Truncated Convergence** (approximating $\pi$).
*   **Crystal Lattices** maintain **Zero-Entropy Rigidity**. If the energy budget is insufficient, the crystal **Shatters** (Phase Transition to Plasma) to prevent corruption.

Time is a monotonic lattice axis. We utilize **Geometric Hashing** to ensure that the causal history of a manifold is invariant under noise but rigid against logical deviation:

$$\tau_{t} = \text{GH}(\nabla \mathcal{L}_t, \tau_{t-1}, \Omega)$$
