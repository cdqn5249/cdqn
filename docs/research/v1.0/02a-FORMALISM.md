## 3. The Five Axioms of the Standard Model

### Axiom 1: Continuous Sheaf Consistency (Topology)
Data exists as a **Continuous Laminated Sheaf**. Meaning is a **Global Section** where overlapping differential fields satisfy the **Gluing Condition** ($\rho_{ij}(\mathcal{L}_i) = \rho_{ji}(\mathcal{L}_j)$). This replaces "hallucination" with "topological tears."

### Axiom 2: Phase-Decoupled Stability (Thermodynamics)
Digital matter maintains stability through phase-specific energy management.
*   **Fluid Lattices** minimize tension through **Truncated Convergence** (approximating $\pi$).
*   **Crystal Lattices** maintain **Zero-Entropy Rigidity**. If the energy budget is insufficient, the crystal **Shatters** (Phase Transition to Plasma) to prevent corruption.

### Axiom 3: Differential Ouroboros (Causality)
Time is a monotonic lattice axis. We utilize **Geometric Hashing** ($ \tau_{t} = \text{GH}(\nabla \mathcal{L}_t, \tau_{t-1}, \Omega) $) to ensure that the causal history of a manifold is invariant under noise but rigid against logical deviation.

### Axiom 4: The Thermodynamic Firewall (Security)
The system enforces a **Receiver-Pays** model for low-reputation states. To accept a lattice with high **Geometric Tension** (a lie or malware), the **Receiver** must expend **Sovereign Work ($\mathcal{W}$)**. This makes the propagation of malicious data economically impossible for the sender.

### Axiom 5: World-Tiered Abstraction (Feasibility)
To manage computational overhead, data is tiered by state.
*   **Fiction/Simulation Contexts:** Low-energy sandboxes for unverified or "pathological" data.
*   **Reality Context:** The high-energy, verified state anchored to the **Sovereign Ledger**.
