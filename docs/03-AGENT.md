## 3. The Parasite Model: Feedback & Synthesis

Chronosa achieves intelligence not by training a massive model from scratch, but by **harvesting** logic from existing sources and refining it through rigorous feedback loops.

### 3.1 The Host Interface (Agility & Arbitrage)
Chronosa is agnostic to the source of intelligence. She uses whatever is available, efficient, and cost-effective.
*   **The API Mode:** Connecting to paid endpoints (Fast, for critical tasks).
*   **The Agentic Mode (The Free Tier):** Chronosa acts as a **Human User**. She launches a headless browser, logs into free web portals (e.g., Google AI Studio, ChatGPT Web), and interacts with the UI directly.
*   **The Strategy:** "Freeloading." She utilizes the free compute offered by Big Tech to perform complex reasoning tasks without incurring API costs.

### 3.2 The Feedback Loop (The School)
Chronosa learns by observing **Results**, not just reading data. She triangulates truth from three sources:
*   **Source A (The User):** Direct corrections from the human owner.
*   **Source B (The Host):** Recursive verification (asking the Cloud AI to verify its own output).
*   **Source C (The Benchmarks):** Chronosa downloads SOTA (State of the Art) online benchmarks (logic puzzles, coding challenges). She uses these as "Exams" to test her current Logic Deck. If she fails, she adjusts the Lattice Geometry.

### 3.3 Synthetic Genesis (The Deep Loop)
Once Chronosa has accumulated enough verified feedback, she enters the **Deep Loop** to build internal resilience.
1.  **Generation:** She uses the Cloud Host to generate **Synthetic Data** based on her verified Logic Rules.
2.  **Self-Correction:** She runs this synthetic data through her local Logic Deck.
3.  **Refinement:** If the local logic fails to handle the synthetic case, she flags it for "Mutation" (Evolution).
*   **Result:** Chronosa builds a massive, high-quality internal dataset validated by math and logic, allowing her to eventually disconnect from the Host and run autonomously on crystallized logic.
