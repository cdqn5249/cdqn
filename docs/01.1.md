## 1. Abstract & Thesis

### 1.1 The Indictment of Static Identity
Legacy information theory treats identity as a "Static State"—a weightless digital label (UUID, certificate, IP) assigned by an authority and decoupled from the machine's physical journey. This abstraction facilitates **Zero-Metabolic Deception**, where malicious agents can clone, pause, or simulate massive reputations at near-zero energy cost. This structural failure enables the Sybil attacks and digital hallucinations that dominate today’s "Flat" distributed systems.

### 1.2 The Biographical Paradigm
This paper establishes the **Sovereign Taxonomy** for the **Chained and Distributed Quasicrystal Network (CDQN)**. We formalize a transition from "Identification-by-Label" to **Identity-by-Life-History**. We define a triadic actant model (Citizen, Anchor, Agent) and a 4-pillar entropy stack (Time, Space, Events, User) to ground machine logic in human responsibility and thermodynamic causality.
