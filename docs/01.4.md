## 4. The Reputability Calculus: Growth and Decay

Reputation is not a tally, but a flux describe by two asymmetric laws.

### 4.1 Logarithmic Accumulation (Growth Law)
Reputability building $(\Delta\Omega_+)$ requires a sustained balance of **Metabolic Effort** $(W)$ and **Verified Lifespan** $(T)$. To maintain dimensional consistency, we utilize reference units $W_0$ and $T_0$.

**Growth Posit [†2: Refined Law]:**
$$\Delta\Omega_{+} \approx \alpha \log\left(\frac{W}{W_{0}}\right) + \beta \log\left(\frac{T}{T_{0}}\right)$$

*   **α / β:** Weighting coefficients $[\alpha + \beta = 1]$ that determine the importance of raw energy versus lived time. 
*   **Sovereign Property:** Logarithmic growth ensures that "Work Bursts" cannot buy high-status. It allows nodes that made "Old Mistakes" to rebuild reputability over a sustained honest duration.

### 4.2 Factorial Decay (Collapse Law)
Reputability loss $(\Delta\Omega_-)$ is instantaneous upon the detection of a Phason (logic fraud).

**Decay Posit [†3: The Cliff]:**
$$\Delta\Omega_{-} = \min\left(\Omega_{\text{current}} - (N_{\text{fraud\_bits}})! \, , \, \Omega_{\text{floor}}\right)$$

*   **The Penalty:** As soon as bits of fraud are identified, the reputation collapses towards $\Omega_{\text{floor}}$ faster than it could ever be built through honest work. 
*   **Reasoning:** Anderson (2020) defines that security protocols must provide "catastrophic outcomes" for adversaries while maintaining "conservative resumption" for legitimate agents.
