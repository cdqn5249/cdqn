## 1. Abstract

This paper serves as the mandatory bridge between actant taxonomy and the physical **Seed-to-Manifold (StM)** process.

*   **Signature:** $\text{Grow}: (W_{t-1}, Q0_t) \to W_t \quad [|W_t| > |W_{t-1}|]$
