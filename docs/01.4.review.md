**Strengths of Logarithmic Growth Model:**

| **Property** | **Mathematical Justification** | **Design Rationale** |
|--------------|-------------------------------|---------------------|
| **Diminishing returns** | $\frac{d}{dW}(\log W) = \frac{1}{W} \to 0$ as $W \to \infty$ | Prevents "buying trust" with computational power |
| **Time-weighted** | $\beta > \alpha$ (stated in §4.1) | Rewards "Lived Presence" over raw energy |
| **Recovery-enabled** | $\log(0)$ undefined, but $W > 0$ always | Allows compromised nodes to rebuild reputation |
| **Bounded growth** | No asymptote to infinity without time | Ensures long-term consistency required |
