## 2. The Atomic Unit: Deriving the Card Data Unit (CDU)

The QS Kernel replaces the weightless "bit" and the "byte" with the **Card Data Unit (CDU)**, the physical tile of the manifold.

### 2.1 The Physicality of Information (Stoichiometric Mass)

*   **Derivation:** If a system allows the infinite duplication of data at zero metabolic cost, it violates the laws of thermodynamics. A sovereign substrate must tie state mutation to energy expenditure proofs to prevent hallucination.
*   **Evidence 1:** Landauer, R. (1961). *Irreversibility and Heat Generation in the Computing Process.* (Establishes the minimum energy cost $k_B T \ln 2$ for information erasure).
*   **Evidence 2:** Vopson, M. M. (2019). *The mass-energy-information equivalence principle.* (Proves information has measurable physical mass-equivalence).

### 2.2 The 128-Byte Axiom (Hardware Atomicity)
*   **Claim:** The CDU must be strictly fixed at 128 bytes to align with hardware cache-line architectures.
*   **Derivation:** To minimize the "Movement Tax," the atomic unit of the OS must be indivisible at the hardware level. High-performance silicon (M-series, ARM Neoverse) utilizes 128-byte cache lines. Fixing the CDU at this size ensures that state, logic, and identity are fetched in a single hardware cycle.
*   **Evidence 1:** Hennessy, J. L., & Patterson, D. A. (2017). *Computer Architecture: A Quantitative Approach.* (Defines performance penalties of cache-line fracturing).
*   **Evidence 2:** Chilimbi, C. M., et al. (1999). *Cache-Conscious Structure Layout.* (Proof that aligning data units to cache blocks eliminates fetch redundancy).

### 2.3 Stoichiometric Partitioning [† Empirical Horizon]
*   **Claim:** The CDU is a 128-byte experienced functional tuple: Provenance ($P$), Payload ($\Sigma$), and Logic ($\Delta$).
*   **Derivation:** To fulfill Capability 10 (Intent Accountability), identity and logic must be physically inseparable from the state. The $P$ partition contains the **Experience Tuple**—the record of all prior topological bonds formed by the CDU.
*   **Explanation:** This reflects the algebraic nature of a **Quantale**—a particle where the logic ($\Delta$) defines the "Bonding Affinity" and $P$ defines the "Causal Order."
*   **Evidence:** **[MISSING INDEPENDENT SOURCE]**. Hardware-enforced 128-byte logic-state-identity atomicity at the kernel layer is a novel QS architectural proposition.
